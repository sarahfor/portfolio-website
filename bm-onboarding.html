Weekly operating rhythm

The role should create calm follow-through across partnerships, content, admin systems, and repeatable AI workflows.

Monday

Review calendar, inbox, partnership tracker, content deadlines, and open decisions. Send Sarah a priority list.

Tuesday

Handle partnership follow-ups, research, pipeline cleanup, and sponsor or media kit support.

Wednesday

Coordinate content operations, drafts, approvals, asset checks, posting schedule, and platform updates.

Thursday

Clean systems, improve SOPs, organize folders, and turn repeated admin into reusable AI workflows.

Friday

Send a status report with completed work, open loops, revenue opportunities, blockers, and next-week priorities.
