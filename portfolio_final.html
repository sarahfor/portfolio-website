About Me

I'm a graduate student in Computer Science specializing in Machine Learning, Generative AI, RLHF, and data quality. My work explores three major questions: How do computational systems drive large-scale change? How does automation and AI reshape labor and workflows? What technical skills are necessary to design, evaluate, and interact with these systems responsibly?

Let's tell ethical technical stories together. I partner with brands to translate complex AI concepts into compelling narratives that educate, inspire, and drive meaningful conversations about technology's role in society.
